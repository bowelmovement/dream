>> Uesugi Tatsuha - Seiyuu

Tatsuha's voice actor is Ishikawa Hideo. He born in Hyougo on December 13th, 1969 and he has blood type O. He is a member of Entertainment Music Unit (E.M.U.) along with Sakaguchi Daisuke (sings Super Drive^^), Okiayu Ryoutarou (the leader), Kanna Nobutoshi, and Midorikawa Hikaru. Some of his other anime roles besides Tatsuha are Masaki in Bubblegum Crisis Tokyo 2040, Ikenami Eiichi in Bannou Bunka Nekomusume(TV), Kenjou Yoshihiko in Boys Be, and Adonis in Cutey Honey. He was also the reporter in episode 143 of Sailormoon o.0 and Aikawa Hiroya in Mahou no Stage Fancy Lala.

Tatsuha...what can I say? He sounds cute and he looks cute.^^ He has a sense of humor and he's fun to watch and listen to .^__^ Ishikawa makes Tatsuha sound hilarious when he's talking about Ryuichi.^_^ He also speaks English pretty well XDD Although it's rare to find a serious Tatsuha, he does do a good job in making him sound serious in those situations. All in all, although we don't hear Tatsuha's voice too often, his voice is a great one to listen to in Gravi as well as Bubblegum Crisis.^^
