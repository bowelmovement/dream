#1) By: Aibon
Regarding: Gravitation DVD 1: Fateful First Encounter
Submitted:July 2nd, 2004

I recently bought myself the Gravitation Volume 1 at Suncoast for $30.00. I thought that the DVD was WAY overpriced.

Boy was I wrong.

After finishing the first episode dubbed I was in love. Shuichi Shindou's voice not only fits him SO well, sometimes I think his voice is much better in English than Japanese!

Some of you must be reading this with faces to marvel at this one: 0_o?! Which I don't blame ANY of you for.

The fact is, Shuichi's voice is what I said to my friend sounds more "dopey." His voice was so hilarious in episode three when he's confessing to Hiro about his night with Yuki Eiri the night before. I nearly wet myself when he said "I won't teeelll~!"

Shuichi's voice is absolutely MARVELOUS and matches almost exactly with the song, so it really does sound like him singing.

But that's my opinion.

Next, Yuki. Yuki, Yuki, Yuki. You have GOT to love Yuki with those come hither eyes, and now, a deep monotone voice to go with those eyes. Yes, fangirls, his voice is also a very good one in the dubbed version too. Nothing though, can amout to Yuki-sama's body. ^_^

I think the only voice I'm not TOO partial to is...None of them~! In fact, i believe that this is the BEST dub I have ever seen. They made scenes FUNNIER. Is that even POSSIBLE?

Yes, i submit it is <3 ^_^

<3 Love always,

Aibon

P.S. Yes. this thing was worth EVERY penny.
