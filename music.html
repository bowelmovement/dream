>> Music
Welcome to the music section of DtD. You will find most Gravitation songs here, as well as variations of some songs. All music are mp3 files and zipped so please make sure you have a mp3 player and Winzip.

Please please only download ONE song at a time. If you download multiple songs simultaneously, it'll run up the bandwidth more and may cause temporary shutdown of the site.

Super Drive
- Gravitation's beginning theme.

Super Drive "Silence" Mix
- Super Drive with total silence and a slower beat.

Glaring Dream
- Gravitation's ending theme.

Predilection NEW
- The newest Gravitation song from the Gravi Sound Track III, sung by Yamaguchi Kappei, Ryuichi's actual seiyuu. xD

Sleepless Beauty
- The most popular song of Nittle Grasper, sold over 1 million copies in Japan.(only claimed in the anime^^) Sung around episode 8.

Shining Collection
- Nittle Grasper's second song, sung by Ryuichi and heard only in the OAVs.

The Rage Beat
- Bad Luck's most popular song, sung several times throughout the series.

Smashing Blue NEW
- The ending theme from the Gravitation OAVs. It's got a superb beat. :3

Blind Game Again
- Another one of Bad Luck's song, the main theme in the OAV series and heard briefly in the anime.

In the Moonlight
- Slow song by Bad Luck, sung by Shuichi and only heard in the OAVs.

Spicy Marmalade
- One of Bad Luck's song, sung in the OAVs and heard briefly in the OAVs during intermissions.

Yuutsu Na Seven Days
- A fun song by Kotani Kinya, sung during episode 10 or so where Yuki takes Shuichi on a date. ^_^
