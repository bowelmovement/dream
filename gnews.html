The Latest Gravitation News

The Feature DVD of NEWTYPE USA in JULY 2004
------------------------------------------------------
NEWTYPE USA, a anime/manga magazine, will have Gravitation as the feature DVD for its July issue. Inside the magazine will be a DVD containing the first episode of Gravitation, dubbed only. NEWTYPE USA may be purchased at your local bookstore as well as online.

The Making of new DRAMA CDs
------------------------------------------------------
According to the Official Gravitation site, 2 new Drama CDs have been released, featuring voices of all characters as well as manga characters such as Rage, Judy Winchester, and Ark. These CDs are now available to purchase online. It has also been announced that the rest of the manga will be made into drama CDs.

The Set Date for the Licensed Gravitation DVD
------------------------------------------------------
According to RightStuf, the first licensed Gravitation DVD - "Gravitation: Fateful First Encounter," is set to appear in local stores on June 29th, 2004 along with a version that comes with a boxset. Both versions of the DVD may now be pre-ordered online.

The License of the MANGA SERIES
------------------------------------------------------
As of early 2003, it has been revealed that the Gravitation manga will be licensed by Tokyopop. Tokyopop has the Gravitation preview section available here, and presently, they will be translating all available volumes of the Gravitation manga.

The License of the ANIME SERIES
------------------------------------------------------
As of Summer 2002, RightStuf announced the fact that they will be licensing the Gravitation anime series. The 2 Gravitation OAVs will also be licensed by RightStuf. A small section of Rightstuf's announcement of the release can be found here.

The Continuation of the original MANGA SERIES
------------------------------------------------------
Maki Murakami has halted her production with the Gravitation manga to concentrate more on her newest project, Kimi no Unajini Kanpai! Presently, the Gravitation manga has halted at the recent release of Volume 12. Continuation of the manga is indefinite.
